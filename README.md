# Welcome to the wiki!

GodMode is a 75/75 Custom Content FFXI Private server.

Exceed the limits and obtain GodHood.

# Server Information

- --server godmode.ddns.net
- 75/75 cap
- Treasures of Aht Urgan content level
- FoV enabled
- GoV enabled
- Survival Guide Teleports Enabled
- Limit Breaks are infused with the Main Scenario Quest (Forced)
- No Subjobs or advanced jobs until your first Rebirth (MSQ)
- Normal exp rates
- Trusts Enabled but limited to 5 (as you Rebirth the amount simultaneously allowed will reduce by one until there is only 1 allowed.)
    - Ferreous Coffin
    - Valaineral
    - Shantotto II
    - Rainmard
    - Monberaux
- Move speed is doubled.
- Craft rates are 9x
- All crafts can go max.
- Fishing is on, but probably doesn't work completely.
- Era Treasure Hunter style.
- Lotteries are set to always spawn.
- Ah bot enabled

# Player Hub

Players can use the !hub command to be teleported to a utility hub.
- Homepoint / Survival Guide 
- Exp camp fast travel book
- Cheat Code book that will do the following:
    - Cap skills
    - Add all spells
    - Add all Songs
    - Add Cor Rolls
    - Add some PUP attachments
    - Add all mounts
    - Add all Chairs
    - Unlock all Weaponskills
    - Complete Rise of the Zilart
    - Complete COP
    - Set Rank to 10
    - Unlock Homepoint Teleports
    - Unlock Survival Guide Teleports

# Main Story Quest

On GodMode there is a narrative driven Main Story Quest (MSQ)

Phoenix in Valhalla (Heaven) has chosen you to be a vessel of power.

<details>
<summary>Chapter 1. Whispers (level 1)</summary>

- Upon entering Vana'diel you are whisked away to valhalla
- Phoenix explains Rebirth and how it works
- Phoenix will test your endurance 
- Tells the player they’re not even remotely ready yet and sends them to Vana’diel to grow on their own.
- Quest Complete
- Player dropped in or near their Home Nation

</details>

<details>
<summary>Chapter 2. Echoes (Level 30)</summary>

- Pleased with your progress you are whisked away to valhalla.
- Phoenix requests that you kill three mini gods near the three crags for him.
- Player is sent back to the previous zone.
- After Killing the three Unnamed Gods you are pulled back to Valhalla
- Phoenix still wants you to get stronger and gives you a set of Auged RSE to aid you.
- You are sent back to the previous zone.
- Quest Complete.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Chapter 3. Fractures (Level 60)</summary>

- Pleased with your progress you are whisked away to valhalla.
- Phoenix requests that you kill the six Avatars to power you further.
- Player is sent back to the previous zone.
- After Killing the six Avatars and collecting the Whispers, you are pulled back to Valhalla
- Phoenix still wants you to get stronger and gives you a set of Auged AF1 to aid you.
- You are sent back to the previous zone.
- Quest Complete.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Chapter 4. Touching The Sky (Level 75)</summary>

- You are whisked away to valhalla.
- Phoenix more rash now asks that you kill Kirin and his underlings.
- Player is sent back to the previous zone.
- After Killing Kirin you are pulled back to Valhalla
- Phoenix Tells you he has no more use for you and needs to end you.
- You are sent to Rala Waterways where Loki is ready to kill you.
- Once Loki is dead, you are brought back to Valhalla.
- The real Phoenix reveals to you that they were in Loki's Soul Prison and you freed them.
- As a reward for your bravery, Phoenix gives you God Power which gives you all the advanced jobs and unlocks subjob capabilities.
- Phoenix give you !valhalla so that you can return any time you please.
- Main Quest Complete.
- At this time you can bring any level 75 job to Phoenix and give him 1,000,000 gil and you will be reset to level 1 with permanent stat boosts.

</details>
